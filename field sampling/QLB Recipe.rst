Queen's Lysis Buffer Preparation
===============================


:Author: Vinay K L.
:Copyright: None
:Date: 10/10/2022

Purpose
-------
To prepare Queen's Lysis Buffer to collect blood samples on field.
-------------------------------------------------------------------

* To 800ml of double distilled water add 1.21g of Tris, 0.58g of Nacl, and 20ml of EDTA( 0.5M, pH 8.0) and 10g n-lauroylsarcosine
Check the pH now and adjust to 8 with HCl if needed and bring the volume to 1L. Once prepared, autoclave the buffer.

* Fill the buffer in Genaxy (Do not use Tarsons) screw cap vials and store in 100 well boxes till used in field.
